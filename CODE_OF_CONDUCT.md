# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the AnyCode community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, caste, colour, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas rather than attacking people.
- Give and receive feedback with care.
- Respect privacy and do not share another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate intimidation,
or the publication of private information without permission are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour privately through a GitHub Security Advisory for
this repository. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits,
issues, and other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.
